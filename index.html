Accountability Rules
1
The 5-Second Pause
Before picking up your phone — "Am I in a check-in window?" If not, put it back.
2
Log it, don't judge it
Every mindless pickup gets logged. Data beats guilt.
3
Phone sleeps outside your room
Eliminates morning scrolling and late-night rabbit holes.
4
Week 1: log, don't restrict
Awareness only. Week 2 you start reducing.
5
Celebrate 3-day streaks
3 days hitting your schedule = reward yourself offline.
6
One free day per week
Life happens. One free day won't reset your streak.
